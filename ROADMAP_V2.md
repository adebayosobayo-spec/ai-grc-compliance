# 🗺️ COMPLAI Roadmap V2 (2026-2027)

Following our successful Vercel deployment and DPO Assist restructuring, here is the strategic focus for the next phases.

## Phase 1: Persistence & Trust (Next 30 Days)
- **Database Persistence**: Move Risk & Asset registers to dedicated Supabase tables (Stop session-only data).
- **Evidence Vault**: Secure file upload for audit evidence with AI-assisted verification.
- **MFA & User Roles**: Role-based access control (RBAC) separating "DPO" (Approver) from "Staff" (Contributors).

## Phase 2: The Multi-Framework Bridge (Q2 2026)
- **Cross-walk Engine**: Map controls between GDPR, NDPR, ISO 27001, and DPDPA. Perform one audit, satisfy multiple frameworks.
- **Automated Threshold Alerts**: AI notifications for when data processing volume triggers mandatory DPO requirements.

## Phase 3: DPO-as-a-Service Ecosystem (Q3-Q4 2026)
- **Marketplace Integration**: Connect organisations directly with verified DPOs globally.
- **Reporting Wizard**: One-click generation of regulator-specific breach notification forms (NITDA, ICO, etc.).
- **Compliance Calendar**: Proactive deadline tracking for policy reviews and internal audits.

## ❓ Areas for Exploration
- **Liability Safeguards**: Legal disclaimers for AI-advisory versus human-professional sign-off.
- **Sector-Specific Modules**: Tailored AI prompts for Fintech, Healthtech, and E-commerce.
