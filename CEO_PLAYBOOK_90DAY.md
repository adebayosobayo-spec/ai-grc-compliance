## DECISION FRAMEWORK: When to Adjust Course

### Signal 1: Revenue Tracking Behind ($10K MRR Not Achieved by Week 12)
- **Investigate**: Is pricing wrong? Product not valuable? Sales/marketing not working?
- **Action**: Customer interviews (10+) to diagnose root cause
- **Pivot Options**:
  - Lower pricing (Starter $19 instead of $29)
  - Shift focus to high-touch sales (target specific verticals like fintech)
  - Double down on partnerships (let others do the distribution)

### Signal 2: Churn Exceeds 5% Monthly
- **Investigate**: Why are customers leaving? (Surveys + exit interviews)
- **Action**: Product improvements based on churn feedback
- **Pivot Options**:
  - Add onboarding/success program (reduce churn 2-3 percentage points)
  - Increase feature velocity (customers churning for missing features)
  - Revisit product-market fit (wrong segment?)

### Signal 3: Partners Not Materializing
- **Investigate**: Is pitch weak? Wrong targets? Bad timing?
- **Action**: Adjust pitch; focus on direct-to-customer instead
- **Pivot Options**:
  - Shift 50% of effort from partnerships to organic/content growth
  - Become white-label for consulting firms (they do distribution)

### Signal 4: Team Struggling to Execute
- **Investigate**: Over-scoped? Unclear priorities? Wrong skills?
- **Action**: Narrow scope. Pick ONE thing. Go deep.
- **Pivot Options**:
  - Extend runway (defer nice-to-haves)
  - Bring in fractional operator/COO to unblock team
  - Hire specialist (e.g., full-stack engineer who moves faster)

## MINDSET: The CEO Job for the Next 90 Days

**You are:** Revenue driver, customer champion, decision-maker, culture-setter.

**You are NOT:** Engineer, marketer, support person (delegate these).

**Your job is:**
1. **Decide** (quickly, with ~70% information, not 100%)
2. **Communicate** (weekly: direction, metrics, wins)
3. **Remove blockers** (if team stuck, unstick them)
4. **Talk to customers** (at least 3 per week)
5. **Make the impossible possible** (hustle, hustle, hustle)

**Measure yourself by:**
- Did we hit revenue goal? (Yes/No)
- Did we ship core features? (Yes/No)
- Are customers happy? (NPS > 45)
- Is team growing in capability? (New engineer productive)
- Do we have clear next steps? (Yes/No)

**Green flags:**
- ✅ Customers asking for features (demand validation)
- ✅ Churn < 5% (product resonates)
- ✅ Partnerships converting (channel working)
- ✅ Team making decisions autonomously (culture forming)
- ✅ Revenue growing 30%+ MoM (flywheel spinning)

**Red flags:**
- ❌ Customers churning without clear reason (misalignment)
- ❌ Team feels stuck/unclear on priorities (leadership issue)
- ❌ No press, no partnerships, no community (not talking about it)
- ❌ CAC > $3K, payback > 4 months (unit economics broken)
- ❌ You're doing sales + engineering + marketing (delegation failure)
